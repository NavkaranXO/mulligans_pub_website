ABOUT MULLIGANS

Mulligans Virtual Golf Pub & Bar offers state-of-the-art golf simulators in a relaxed pub atmosphere. Whether you're a seasoned golfer looking to improve your game or a beginner wanting to try something new, our facility provides the perfect environment.

Our premium simulators feature a variety of courses, detailed analytics, and realistic gameplay. Enjoy a cold beer, delicious food, and great company while you play!

FAQS

Do I need to bring my own clubs?

While you're welcome to bring your own clubs, we offer complimentary club rentals for those who need them. We have options for right and left-handed players of all skill levels.

Can I bring my own food and drinks?

Outside food and beverages are not permitted as we offer a full menu of food and drinks that can be delivered directly to your simulator bay.

How do I make a reservation?

Call or text 236-425-3341. You can also contact us here.

Do you host events?

We love to host parties, corporate events, and more. We can comfortably accomodate up to 100 people. Contact us to book your event today.

What does it cost to play pool or darts?

Pool and darts are free! Come enjoy our menu and games!
